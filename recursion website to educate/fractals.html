Understanding Fractals and Recursion

Fractals are fascinating geometric shapes that can be generated using recursive processes. They are prevalent in mathematics, computer graphics, and nature itself. A fractal is a self-replicating pattern that exhibits intricate detail at every level of magnification.

The Mandelbrot Set

The Mandelbrot Set is one of the most famous fractals. It's generated using a recursive process called an iterative algorithm. The result is a mesmerizing image of a complex and infinitely intricate shape.
