Computer Science Career Paths

Software Developer:

Design and create applications, software, and systems to solve real-world problems.

Data Scientist:

Analyze and interpret data to extract valuable insights and make data-driven decisions.

Web Developer:

Build and maintain websites and web applications for businesses and individuals.

DevOps Engineer:

Streamline software development and deployment processes through automation and collaboration.
